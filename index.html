Michael Cazares

Aspiring Web Developer

Born in Fort Clayton, Panama Canal Zone in 1954.
In 1955, I arrived and was raised in the Boyle Heights section of (East) Los Angeles.
In 1970, first job at McDonald's, earning $1.35 per hour.
Enlisted as a volunteer in the United States Marine Corps in 1972.
Embassy Marine in Berlin, Germany, 1976.
In 1977, discharged and enrolled at Cal State University Los Angeles.
1981 - Married Roseann Millan, my fellow classmate at CSULA. Still married to her. :-)
1984 - Served as a Superior Court Clerk, in both criminal and civil courtrooms.
1995 - Worked at EarthLink (ISP) as a technical manager.
2009 - A high school math teacher for Los Angeles Unified School District.
2023 - Retired from Los Angeles Unified School District.
2023 - Mathematics instructor for TELACU's Veterans Upward Bound program.

Today - A hopeful applicant of Vets Who Code
